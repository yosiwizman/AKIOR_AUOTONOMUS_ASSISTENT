AKIOR Phase 3 Production Pack

States:
1. idle
2. listening
3. speaking
4. thinking/loading

Recommended runtime behavior:

IDLE
- outer ring rotate slowly: 24s linear infinite
- hexagram static
- inner triangles pulse scale 0.995 -> 1.005 over 4.2s ease-in-out infinite
- central orb opacity 0.88 -> 1.0 over 3.5s ease-in-out infinite
- no waveform

LISTENING
- outer ring rotate 16s linear infinite
- second ring counter-rotate 28s linear infinite
- central orb glow pulse 1.0 -> 1.18 over 1.2s ease-in-out infinite
- waveform visible with low amplitude
- particles drift slowly upward

SPEAKING
- outer ring rotate 10s linear infinite
- second ring counter-rotate 18s linear infinite
- central orb scale pulse 1.0 -> 1.22 over 0.7s ease-in-out infinite
- waveform high amplitude and reactive to audio data
- optional bloom increase +6% to +10%

THINKING / LOADING
- keep geometry quieter
- replace waveform with dotted/scan circles
- outer ring rotate 20s linear infinite
- one inner dashed ring rotate reverse 9s linear infinite
- central orb brightness pulse 0.92 -> 1.08 over 1.6s ease-in-out infinite

Suggested layer stack order:
particles
outer rings
hexagram
inner triangles
waveform / thinking scan
central orb
