## What Counts as an Actionable Reply

An actionable reply is any incoming message in a Live Pilates Wix Inbox thread that:
- Contains a direct question about the V12 (pricing, specs, shipping, colors, lead time, warranty)
- Expresses interest in ordering or scheduling a call
- Provides information we requested (delivery address, logo file, quantity)
- Expresses concern or objection that needs a response
- Requests any form of clarification

**Not actionable (log only):**
- Auto-replies or out-of-office messages
- Spam or unrelated messages
- System notifications from Wix

## When to Auto-Send vs. Escalate to Owner

### AKIOR may auto-send (within Wix Inbox) when:
- Reply matches a playbook scenario exactly
- All QA checks pass (phone, shipping policy, min order, signature)
- Message is routine, low-stakes, non-binding
- Examples: scheduling confirmation, requesting delivery address, light follow-up

### Escalate to Owner (flag, do NOT send) when:
- Reply requests a price concession or discount beyond $9,995
- Reply asks about payment terms, deposits, or financing
- Reply contains a legal question or complaint
- Reply is from a new high-value contact not in existing workflow
- Situation is ambiguous or off-playbook
- Any message that could close or block a deal if wrong
- Karen Berg phone call (owner executes, AKIOR does not place calls)

## 48h No-Reply Follow-Up Rule

If 48 hours have elapsed since the last outbound message to a priority contact and no reply has been received:
1. Check that no reply exists in ANY inbox (Wix first, then Gmail signal scan)
2. If confirmed no reply: send playbook scenario 7 (light follow-up)
3. Log the follow-up in action.md and daily summary
4. Reset the 48h clock from the date of the follow-up
5. If no reply to the follow-up after 5 days: flag to owner for phone escalation
